Facial Coverings

Face masks protect yourself and others from COVID-19 by creating a barrier between your
mouth and nose and those around you. This makes it more difficult for the droplets which spread
COVID-19 thorugh coughs, sneezes, and talking to reach other people.
Wearing a face mask correctly is vital to deacrease the spread of the virus.
